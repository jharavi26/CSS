CSS Position Property Demonstration

Static Position (Default)

I am a static element. I follow the normal document flow.

Relative Position

I am a relative element. I am offset by 20px from my original position.

Absolute Position

I am an absolute element. I am positioned relative to my nearest positioned ancestor or the viewport if none exists.

Fixed Position

I am a fixed element. I stay in the same position relative to the viewport when you scroll.

Sticky Position

I am a sticky element. I stick to the top of the container when you scroll.

Scroll down inside this container to see the sticky behavior.

Sticky positioning is useful for creating sticky headers or navigation bars.

Keep scrolling to see how it works!

Sticky elements combine characteristics of relative and fixed positioning.
